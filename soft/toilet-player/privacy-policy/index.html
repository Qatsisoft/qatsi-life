Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toilet Player! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Toilet Player does not require you to create an account, does not track your location, and does not access your contacts or photos (beyond the storage permission needed to play your own music files).

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Light Sensor: Used solely to detect ambient light levels to start/stop playback. No data leaves your device.
  • Internet Access: Required only to stream radio stations (SomaFM, World Radio) and download station lists.
  • Storage Access: Required to play local MP3 files you select from your device.
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the light sensor active and music playing when the screen is off.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may connect to third-party services for streaming content:

  • SomaFM: When you select SomaFM as a source, the app connects directly to SomaFM servers to fetch station lists and audio streams. Please refer to SomaFM's Privacy Policy.
  • Radio Browser API: When using World Radio, the app queries the community-driven Radio Browser database.

5. Advertising and Analytics

Toilet Player does not contain any third-party advertisements or analytics SDKs (like Google Analytics or Firebase). Your usage data remains private on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at qatsi.hotline@gmail.com
