Toilet Player

Give Your Old Smartphone a New Purpose!

Transform that spare Android phone gathering dust in a drawer into a dedicated, automatic ambient music player for your bathroom (or any room!). Toilet Player uses the built-in light sensor to detect when you enter the room. Turn on the lights, and soothing music starts playing automatically. Turn them off, and it gently fades out. No buttons to press, no apps to launch—it just works.

Perfect For:

  • Bathrooms: Create a spa-like atmosphere instantly.
  • Reading Nooks: Auto-play focus music when you sit down.
  • Entryways: Welcome guests with music as they arrive.
  • Repurposing Old Tech: The best way to use an old smartphone that's always plugged in.

Key Features:

  • Light-Triggered Playback: Starts when lights go on, stops when they go off.
  • Multiple Audio Sources:
    • Local MP3s: Play your own curated folder of songs.
    • Ambient Loops: Choose from built-in relaxing sounds like River Stream and Spring Birds.
    • SomaFM Integration: Stream ad-free ambient radio stations like Groove Salad and Drone Zone (Premium).
    • World Radio: Access thousands of radio stations globally (Premium).
  • Smart Fallback: If the internet cuts out, it automatically switches to your local fallback tracks so the music never stops.
  • Customizable: Adjust light sensitivity, fade-in/out duration, and playback volume.
  • Designed to Run Forever: Optimized for devices that are permanently plugged into a charger.
How to Use:
  1. Plug your old phone into a charger and place it in the room (e.g., on a shelf or mounted to the wall).
  2. Open Toilet Player and select your music source.
  3. Set the light sensitivity threshold so it detects your room's light.
  4. Leave it and enjoy! The app monitors the sensor efficiently in the background.

Note: This app is designed for devices that are stationary and connected to power. It uses the light sensor continuously to provide instant response.
